About me

Hello! I'm Gilles, a passionate web developer with a love for creating immersive online experiences. My journey in the world of web development began last year, after fifteen years as a toolmaker in the industry and since then, I've been on a mission to bring innovative digital solutions to life. Here's a little more about me:
I'm truly passionate about creating beautiful and functional websites and web applications. I find joy in turning ideas into interactive, user-friendly, and visually appealing digital products.
I specialize in a range of web development technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node JS and React. I'm experienced in both front-end and back-end development, which allows me to provide end-to-end solutions for my clients.
I believe in the power of collaboration and open communication. I work closely with clients to understand their unique needs, and I'm dedicated to delivering tailored solutions that resonate with their vision. I'm a problem solver, always eager to take on new challenges and learn from them.
I'm always open to new opportunities, collaborations, and exciting projects. If you're looking for a passionate web developer to bring your ideas to life, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm just an email away.
